## Pricing Philosophy

### Before Maps
- **Three-tier transparent pricing:** New destinations cost more (scouting investment), repeat routes cost less
- Small groups (8–12) to maintain quality
- Application-based — not open booking

### Ms Paul Therapies
- Per-session therapy fees (configured in Sanity `siteConfig`)
- Free discovery call (Cal.com)
- Digital products at accessible price points
